A BMI calculator is a tool used to assess your Body Mass Index (BMI), a measure that relates your weight to your height. By inputting your weight and height into the calculator, it calculates your BMI, providing a numerical value that indicates whether your weight falls within a healthy range relative to your height. This helps you understand your overall health status and whether you may be underweight, normal weight, overweight, or obese, allowing for informed decisions about your health and wellness.
